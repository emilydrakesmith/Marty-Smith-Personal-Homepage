About Marty

Like a chef who knows that proper measurements are no substitute for good taste and a sharp palette, I'm a full stack developer who applies a great deal of customization and attention to even the most minute detail to my projects. No two builds are the same nor are the needs of two different clients, and I take great pride in producing outstanding, innovative work for all my creations. In addition to my work as a developer, I've spent significant time in the automotive and biotech industries. My formal education is in Public Health and Integrative Biology.

Programming is a team sport and I'm always looking for new collaborators, projects, and opportunities. If you'd like to work with me so we can grow our skills as a team or you'd like to add my talents to your organization don't hesitate to reach out to me. I'm also available to your organization as a consultant and actively seeking volunteer opportunities for causes I believe in.

Technologies I'm proficient with include:

  • JavaScript ES6+ — Node.js/Express.js/EJS — React — Next.js — jQuery — MongoDB — PostgreSQL — Python/Django

I believe strongly in the importance of key design principles:

Portfolio

ScriptStud.io

Description: Every developer consults the Google for troubleshooting code, but most sources where users share and compare code snippets are designed around sharing code which is broken, non-functioning, or in other ways problemative. ScriptStud.io is a website by and for developers to document, save, and share examples of well-written functional code. This was a collaborative project

Technical Details: This is a full MERN-stack application and its construction was and continues to be a collaborative effort between myself and fellow developer Christian Mosley. This app features React extensions for JS code processing and syntax highlighting as well as rendering on the front-end.

Skills: React, Node/Express.js, MongoDB, JSON Web Tokens, Tailwinds, Prism.js

Job Hound

Description: Job Hound helps you track those apps! A modern job search is vastly more complex than the same process just 20 years ago. In a process where a young professional might only accept a position after hundreds of applications, Job Hound is an application to keep the process organized. A young professional hunting for a job can use it to log vital data about positions and companies they've applied to and track follow-up activity on those applications.

Technical Details: This full-stack application was built as a MEN-stack as an effort to make a project with entirely server-side processing. It secures users' data by using Google OAuth. This project was developed individually by myself.

Skills: Node/Express.js, MongoDB, OAuth (Google)

Link to GitHub Repo • Job Hound Live App on Heroku

SoQuiz.me
