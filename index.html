About Marty

Like a chef who knows that proper measurements are no substitute for good taste and a sharp palette, I'm a full stack developer who applies a great deal of customization and attention to even the most minute detail to my projects. No two builds are the same nor are the needs of two different clients, and I take great pride in producing outstanding, innovative work for all my creations. In addition to my work as a developer, I've spent significant time in the automotive and biotech industries.

Programming is a team sport and I'm always looking for new collaborators, projects, and opportunities. If you'd like to work with me so we can grow our skills as a team or you'd like to add my talents to your organization don't hesitate to reach out to me. I'm also available to your organization as a consultant and actively seeking volunteer opportunities for causes I believe in.

Languages I'm proficient with include:

  • HTML5 & CSS
  • JavaScript (ES6+)
  • C++

When not locked in front of my computer working on a project, I can be found enjoying a good movie, taking the trailer on a camping trip with my family, barbecuing a pork shoulder in the back yard, or complaining to the TV about the New York Rangers' power play. I prefers my steak rare, takes my gin with tonic and rosemary, and will accept a meatball cookoff challenge from anyone. My favorite homes away from home are New Orleans and Cape Cod.
